Bradford Insight Network

For AI Teams

Clients sometimes bring highly structured requests, including schemas, rubrics, or predefined inputs. Bradford Insight Network does not design, refine, or validate these structures. When structured work is involved, it is defined by the client or the expert and executed directly by the expert. Bradford Insight Network's role is limited to facilitating the relationship and coordinating the engagement mechanics, not managing or interpreting the resulting outputs.

This approach preserves expert independence, avoids false precision, and ensures that responsibility for conclusions and outcomes remains where it belongs.
