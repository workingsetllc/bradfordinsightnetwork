Bradford Insight Network

For AI Teams

Clients sometimes bring highly structured requests, including predefined schemas, rubrics, or labeling tasks. Bradford Insight Network does not design, refine, or validate these structures. When structured work is involved, it is defined by the client or the expert and executed directly by the expert.

Bradford Insight Network facilitates the relationship and engagement logistics, but does not review, aggregate, or interpret expert outputs. This preserves expert independence and ensures that responsibility for conclusions and outcomes remains with the appropriate parties.
