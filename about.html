Bradford Insight Network

About

Unlike traditional expert networks or consultancies, Bradford Insight Network does not attempt to productize expert judgment or impose a house methodology. We work with senior, reputation-sensitive experts who value autonomy and credibility, and with clients who want direct access to real practitioners rather than abstracted opinions.

Depending on the engagement, experts may work through Bradford Insight Network or contract directly with clients. In all cases, our role remains the same: matching the right person to the ask, facilitating the engagement, and staying out of the substance of the work itself.
