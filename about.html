About

Most expert networks optimize for scale. Bradford Insight Network optimizes for trust.

Our model is grounded in the reality that the most valuable experts—those whose judgment actually matters—are selective about where and how they engage. Many will only work through people they already trust. Bradford Insight Network exists to make those connections possible, responsibly and without forcing expert judgment into artificial structures or platform-driven workflows.

Depending on the engagement, experts may work through Bradford Insight Network or contract directly with clients. In all cases, Bradford Insight Network's role is the same: identifying the right expert for the ask, facilitating the engagement, and staying out of the substance of the work itself.
