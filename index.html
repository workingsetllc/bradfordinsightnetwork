Bradford Insight Network is built on relationships that don't exist anywhere else.

Elite Access, Vetted by Reputation

Co-founded by Rob Bradford, whose trust network spans decades at the highest levels of professional baseball, we connect teams with evaluators and practitioners who typically operate off-market. Our work starts with knowing — credibly — who the right human is for a specific ask.

Eric oversees client coordination and delivery end to end, ensuring that expert insight is captured clearly, delivered reliably, and ready for immediate use.

From Judgment to Usable Insight
